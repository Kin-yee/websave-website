Analytics and ads

Websave collects aggregate, anonymous statistics, such as the percentage of users who use specific features, to improve our application. We automatically collect certain non-personal information when you visit, use, or navigate the Websave website and app. This information includes device and usage details like your device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, device name, country, coarse location, and other technical information. We primarily use this information for maintaining the security, performance, stability and operation of our applications and for internal analytics and reporting. Websave uses services provided by Mixpanel, a business analytics service company. Mixpanel provides us with the means to track user interactions within our apps. The tracked data is anonymous and used to identify usage trends, not individual usage. The data collected includes information about how and when you use our apps, which helps us make improvements to our services. For more information about Mixpanel's privacy practices, you can read Mixpanel's Privacy Policy.
